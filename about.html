    About

    Introducing...SaveDuck! A twist on the classic word-guessing game. Do you like ducks? Ever felt that Hangman was a tad too dark? Delve into a world where every correct guess not only reveals the mystery word but also rescues an adorable duck. Join in this delightful journey where fun meets compassion. Experience all of the same fun and guess correctly to save the duck!
